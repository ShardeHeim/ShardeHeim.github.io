Scrunch

Roles
UX Design, UI Design, Front End Development, Content
Time
September 2017 - Febuary 2019

Scrunch is an Influencer Marketing Platform that matches 20 million influencers to brands and agencies globally. Scrunch’s Web App works as a directory to refine and filter down influencers based on a range of criteria, including their location, follower range, age, gender, audience ect. You can also run your own marketing campaign on the Scrunch platform.

During my time at Scrunch, I worked on a number of projects including redesigning the end to end web app experience, a mobile app designed just for Influencers, skeleton personas, front end development and design for the Scrunch website, preset development and general content for the company.
