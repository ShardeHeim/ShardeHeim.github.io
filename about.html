ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Shardé, a designer who codes. I recently moved from Brisbane to Sydney, Australia.

My goal is to be a part of a project that creates a positive impact in the world. I want to be testing the boundary of new technologies, new methods and not be confined by the way it ‘has been done’.

I am particularly passionate about situating myself in the overlap between user-centred design and software, and the methods by which that transition can be made most seamlessly.

I have developed skills that allow me to take ideas from a concept, through research, mock-ups, prototypes, and testing to functional end products. I see myself transitioning more into the Product Design space as I grow my business knowledge.

I also enjoy reading, re-watching Harry Potter for the nth time, eating giant doughnuts and running my dog’s Instagram page.
